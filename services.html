Our Services

From test drives to trade-ins and nationwide delivery — we make buying your next car effortless.

🚗

Test Drives

Experience any vehicle in our showroom before you commit. Our team will set up a personalised test drive at your convenience — no pressure, no obligation. Available 7 days a week at our Nairobi showroom.

💳

Financing Assistance

Our in-house finance team works with Kenya's leading banks and credit providers to secure the best rates for you. We guide you through the entire process — from application to approval.

🔄

Trade-In Programme

Upgrade to your next car with ease. We offer competitive valuations for your current vehicle and deduct the amount directly from your purchase. Transparent, fair, and instant.

🚚

Nationwide Delivery

No matter where you are in Kenya, we can deliver your car to your doorstep. Our logistics partners ensure safe, insured, and timely delivery across all 47 counties.

🔧

Vehicle Inspection

Every pre-owned vehicle in our inventory undergoes a comprehensive 120-point inspection. We provide the full service history and inspection report so you buy with confidence.

⚙

After-Sales Support

Our relationship doesn't end at sale. We provide after-sales support including warranty assistance, service scheduling, and genuine parts sourcing for your peace of mind.
